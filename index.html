Chapter One

Our Story

“Every chapter led us here.”

May 2022

It All Started With a Dance

Four years ago, Michael asked me to dance. As he patiently tried to teach me, he asked why I never danced with anyone. I told him I had never really learned how to dance with a partner. Dancing with my mom in the kitchen wasn’t quite the same, and neither was dancing in my dad’s arms when I was little.

He laughed, and somehow what started as a dance turned into a conversation that felt like we had already been friends for years.

July 26, 2025

His Version

When asked what he considered our first date, Michael said it was the day we went to the beach with my mom.

Simple, sweet, and so perfectly us.

February 8, 2026

A Place at the Table

For me, our first official date was on Super Bowl Sunday, just a couple of days after we made things official. What made that day special wasn’t the game itself. It was being surrounded by the people he loved.

I got to see the family that helped shape the man I was falling in love with, and little by little, I began to understand why Michael loves the way he does.
